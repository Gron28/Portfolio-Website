About Me

I’m Felipe Villada, a 21-year-old multidisciplinary designer and artist from Argentina. I've always been drawn to "creating" in some way, whether through art, design, or development. I love bringing ideas to life, from drawing tiny creatures as a kid and recognizing their unique personalities to exploring 2D animation, 3D modeling, game development, and even robotics.

I grew up in a small town surrounded by nature, which sparked a deep fascination with biology, ecology, and observing the world around me. I like frogs, collecting fossils, and dismantling old electronics. I enjoy caring for plants and animals, as well as exploring philosophy, psychology, and other studies of Nature that challenge old perspectives and deepen my understanding of life and creativity.

In 2022, I faced a profound personal crisis, hitting rock bottom after struggling with severe depression and agoraphobia. It became a turning point, forcing me to confront and rebuild from within, learning to truly listen to myself and move forward. Today, I focus on practices that help me grow mentally, emotionally, and creatively. I haven’t felt depressed since that turning point; in fact, I’ve never felt so alive, finding joy in everyday life. So I now add that my perspective is genuinely optimistic.

I'm excited to continue learning, exploring, and creating, and I’m eager to make a living through my work. I look forward to collaborating with others who share my passion for bringing meaningful projects to life. I really am.
