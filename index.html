About Me

I’m Felipe Villada, a 21-year-old artist from Argentina. This website/portfolio is a kind of talisman of a part of my work up until now, reflecting my journey as a creative. It will only grow and get better from here.

I've always been drawn to "creating" in some kind of way, whether through code, design, or art. I love to give life to ideas, from drawing tiny creatures as a kid and recognizing their unique personalities to going a step further and learning 2D animation, then 3D modeling, game development, and even robotics.

My interests span from biology and ecology (I grew up in a small town, where I spent my infancy immersed in nature) to caring for plants and animals, and ultimately, caring for myself. I delve into different teachings in philosophy, psychology and other studies of Nature, seeking to understand the world and to smoothly express and enjoy my place in it. I like taking apart old electronics, collecting rocks and fossils, the color green, frogs and exploring new ideas that brake apart my old perspectives.

Around 2020, during the COVID-19 pandemic, I collapsed under some long growing deep issues that culminated with agoraphobia and really severe depression. Today I could say that I am truly grateful of having been through so much, It forced me to bring everything out and to eventually learn to truly listen to myself and to move forwards, and hitting rock bottom, I had nowhere else to go but upwards. Now I must say that I haven't felt depressed in over 2 years, I would even say that I never felt so alive, finding joy in everyday life. so I would now add that my perspective is genuinely optimistic.

I'm excited to continue learning, exploring, and creating, and I’m eager to make a living through my work. I look forward to collaborating with others who share my passion for bringing meaningful projects to life. I really am.
