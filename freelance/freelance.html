How it Works

Simple. Transparent. Fast.

I send you a visual preview. If you like the direction, we move forward. If not, I adjust it or we part ways. No cost either way.

3

You Decide to Start

Choose your package. Pay 50% deposit. I take the mockup and build the real site based on your final input.

You get a staging link to see progress. Request changes (within your revision limit). I refine until you approve.

Your Questions, Answered

Is the Mockup really free?

Yes. I design a visual preview in 24hs. If you don't like it, you don't pay anything. No risk.

What do you need from me to start?

After you see and approve the mockup, I'll schedule a call or send a short brief (5 questions) and request a 50% deposit to reserve your date. Then we start building.

Do I own the website?

100%. If you choose the monthly management, I handle it. But if you want to leave, I give you all the files.

Can we add more features later?

Absolutely. This is not a template. It can grow as much as your business needs.
