Your Questions, Answered

What do you need from me to get started?

After the short form, I’ll send you a simple Project Brief where you share your goals, contact info, and business details. The clearer the info, the closer we get to your vision.

What if my content (text/images) isn’t ready yet?

No problem. The 7-day timeline only starts when the Brief is complete. I can help refine your text, but having good images ready is highly recommended.

What if I already have a logo or branding?

Great. We’ll integrate your brand for consistency, and use the saved time for another feature like a gallery or basic SEO.

Can the 7-day process be delayed?

It depends on our collaboration. To keep the speed, I’ll need your feedback within 24 hours in each revision round.

What happens when the project is finished?

The website is fully yours with 100% ownership and no lock-in. I include one week of technical support, and can offer optional maintenance plans afterward.
